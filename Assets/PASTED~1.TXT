1. Startup Concept

The startup idea is an organization named Pet Care, whose main objective is to help and rescue unclaimed and abandoned street animals. The organization provides food, shelter, medical care, grooming, and adoption facilities to rescued animals.

2. Problem & Solution
Problem

There are many unclaimed animals on the streets with:

No food

No shelter

No care

No medical assistance

Solution

Pet Care will:

Rescue unclaimed animals

Bring them to the Pet Care shelter

Provide medical support, food, and grooming

Keep them in the facility

Make them available for adoption

Provide paid services for domestic pet owners

3. Features of the Platform

The platform will allow:

Posting rescue requests (only for logged-in users)

Selling rescued animals to people who want to adopt them

Selling pet-related products (food, shelter items, clothes, toys)

Paid pet-care services such as:

Pet feeding

Grooming

Short-term or long-term stay

Booking system for pet-care appointments

Online payment option (dummy — no real API key needed)

4. Revenue Model

The startup will generate income by:

Selling adopted pets to people who want to take care of them

Selling pet-care products (food, toys, clothes, shelter items, etc.)

Paid pet-care services where users can book:

Pet feeding packages

Grooming packages

Hourly care packages

Full-day care plans
