About Pet Care

Our Mission

Pet Care is dedicated to rescuing abandoned and unclaimed street animals, providing them with medical care, shelter, and finding them loving homes.

Founded with a passion for animal welfare, our organization has grown to become a trusted name in animal rescue and adoption. We believe every animal deserves a second chance at life, and we work tirelessly to make that happen.
